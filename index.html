Deployment Strategies

Deployment process is the most crucial part of any product release cycle, minor mistake can lead to disasters. To avoid this, a guidline is to be followed

Create a new release tag

As part of the nomenculature that will give an idea of four wh's

  • Where?
  • When?
  • What?
  • Why?

consider a tag prod_21032014_32 and prod_24032014_32_hf

  • The first tag answers three wh's
    • It was deployed on production server.
    • It was deployed on 21, March, 2013.
    • Release for sprint 32.
  • The second tag answers four wh's.
    • It was deployed on production server.
    • It was deployed on 24, March, 2013.
    • Release for sprint 32.
    • It is a hot fix for Sprint 32.

Steps to follow after production deployment.

  • Login to acquia web interface.
    • clear varnish cache.
  • Login to acquia servers through ssh.
    • clear drupal cache using drush @site.environment cc all.

How to do production hotfix.

  • Checkout the current production tag.
  • Do the code changes.
  • Commit the changes.
  • git -a tag new tag -m "new message"
  • git push origin new tag

My Company

Background

Srijan is a 12 year old organisation providing solutions around enterprise web content management and consultation. It's presence is spread across different geographical location from Delhi, Dharamsala and Goa in India to Delaware in USA.

Srijan is serving clients across Asia, Europe, United States and Middle East.

Strengths

Srijan strongly believes in the following idealogy

  • Democracy at work place.
  • Transperancy.
  • No heirarchy.
  • Alignment of personal and professional growth and goals.

Everyone is allowed to express their thoughts, discuss problems and raise issues, which are addressed in the best possible manner. This was achieved by removing the barriers of heirarchy in the company, where everyone is treated with equality.

Work culture

With great power, comes great responsibility. Holding each other accountable to see through the success of a project is the key expectation from each member. While there is no heirarchy, but there are sense of responsibility expected from each role to be performed and fulfilled.

There is a transparent system, which allows everyone to know each other's salary. The concept of openbooks allows any member of the organization to look at the profit and loss account of the company. There is continious 360 degree evaluation system, which allows an individual to improve by working on the areas of weakness.

Srijan also promotes the concepts of the employee owned company by releasing a percentage of share to the members of organization.

Conclusion

The sharing and caring nature of the members of Srijan makes it a unqiue workplace working towards a common goal that aligns with the personal goal. While every member puts in his best effort to acheive excellence, but sometime the goals are not accomplished, As no one is perfect, rather then giving up people learn from their mistakes.
